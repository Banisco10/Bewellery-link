Bewellery Etoile

Elegant jewelry, delivered to your door.

About the App

Bewellery Etoile is a mobile shopping application that lets customers browse, purchase, and track orders for jewelry — including rings, necklaces, bracelets, and earrings — from the convenience of their phone.

What You Can Do

  • Browse a curated catalogue of jewelry products with detailed descriptions and images
  • Create an account using email or Google Sign-In
  • Save favorite items to a wishlist
  • Place orders and pay securely online or choose cash on delivery
  • Track order status from confirmation through delivery
  • Leave reviews on products you've purchased
  • Contact customer support directly through in-app live chat

Who It's For

Bewellery Etoile is built for customers in Ghana looking for a simple, trustworthy way to shop for jewelry online, with clear order tracking and responsive customer support at every step.
