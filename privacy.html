Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bewellery Etoile. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information including your full name, email address, phone number, profile information, delivery addresses, order history, wishlist information, product reviews, and customer support messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to create and manage your account, process orders, complete payments, arrange deliveries, provide customer support, improve our services, and send order-related notifications.

4. Authentication

Bewellery Etoile supports email authentication and Google Sign-In. When you use Google Sign-In, we may receive information such as your name, email address, and profile picture for account management.

5. Payments

Payments are processed securely through Paystack. Bewellery Etoile does not store complete payment details such as card numbers or mobile money PINs.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services including Supabase, Google services, Paystack, Expo services, and email providers to provide authentication, storage, payments, and communication features.

7. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion. Personal account information may be removed, while certain records such as completed orders and transaction records may be retained for business and legal purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Users under 18 may use Bewellery Etoile with the permission and involvement of a parent or legal guardian.
