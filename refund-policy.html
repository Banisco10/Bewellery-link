BeWellery Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

At BeWellery, customer satisfaction is important to us. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help. Please read our Refund & Return Policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Return Window

Customers may request a return within 3 days from the date the order is marked as delivered.

Requests submitted after this period may not be eligible for a refund or exchange.

2. Eligible Items

To qualify for a return, the item must:

  • Be unused and unworn.
  • Be in its original condition.
  • Include all original packaging, tags, certificates (if provided), and accessories.
  • Show no signs of damage caused after delivery.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged, defective, or incorrect:

  • Customized or personalized jewelry.
  • Earrings (for hygiene reasons).
  • Gift cards or promotional items.
  • Items marked as Final Sale or Non-Returnable.

4. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you receive a damaged item, defective product, or the wrong item, please contact BeWellery Customer Support within 24 hours of delivery.

Please provide:

  • Your order number.
  • Clear photographs of the item.
  • Photographs of the packaging.

5. Refund Process

Once your returned item has been received and inspected, we will notify you of the outcome of your refund request.

  • Approved refunds will be issued using the original payment method whenever possible.
  • Refund processing times depend on your payment provider.
  • If your return does not meet our policy requirements, your refund request may be declined.

6. Shipping Costs

Original shipping charges are generally non-refundable.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless:

  • The wrong item was delivered.
  • The product is defective.
  • The item arrived damaged.

7. Return Approval

Returns should not be sent without first requesting approval through BeWellery.

Once your request has been approved, you will receive instructions on how and where to return your item.

8. Order Cancellation

Orders may only be cancelled before they have been processed or shipped.

Once an order has been dispatched, it can only be returned through the normal return process.

9. Fraud Prevention

BeWellery reserves the right to deny refund or return requests that appear fraudulent, abusive, or inconsistent with this policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding returns or refunds, please contact BeWellery Customer Support through the Support section of the BeWellery mobile application.

By placing an order on BeWellery, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Refund & Return Policy.
